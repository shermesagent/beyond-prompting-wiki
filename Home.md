## What This Is

This wiki is about one thing: helping people move **beyond prompting** — from treating AI like a chat bot to treating it like a capable teammate you can delegate to.

It's built for:
- **Teachers** who want AI to do more than write lesson plans
- **Leaders** who sense there's a bigger play than just "better prompts"
- **Anyone** who's ready to stop typing questions and start designing systems

The concepts here are based on real research — papers, case studies, practitioner experience — but distilled into plain language. No academic gatekeeping. No jargon without explanation.
