# AI Coaching for Accelerating Human Skill Development with Reinforcement Learning

Wei Wang, Enlin Gu, Antonio Loquercio, Haimin Hu, Rahul Mangharam
arXiv:2606.25337, June 25, 2026

**Key findings:**
- AI copilots can substantially boost human performance through shared control, but excessive assistance can induce over-reliance and skill atrophy.
- Effective coaching requires strategic scaffolding and stepping back aligned with learner's capability — allowing productive failures that drive learning.
- Formalized as non-cooperative dynamic game: learner optimizes task performance, coach targets learner's independent competence.
- RL framework combining adaptive shared control with probabilistic models of coach's causal influence on skill evolution.
- User study (N=33) on first-person-view drone racing: significant gains in human learning outcomes over state-of-the-art AI coaching baselines.
