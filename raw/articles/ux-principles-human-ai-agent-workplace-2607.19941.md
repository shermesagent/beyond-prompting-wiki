# A Framework of User Experience Principles for Human-AI Agent Interaction in the Workplace

arXiv:2607.19941, July 23, 2026

As AI agents become integral to business workflows, establishing guiding user experience (UX) principles is crucial for ensuring user trust and successful adoption. This study uses a multi-method approach — combining participatory design workshop, paper-and-pencil, expert review, meta-analysis, and in-depth interviews — to identify and validate a design framework of eight core UX principles for human-AI agent interaction in the workplace.

Key findings:
- Eight core UX principles identified and validated for human-AI agent interaction
- Principles provide actionable guardrails for designers and software engineers
- Creates a structured foundation for future empirical studies on agentic AI in enterprise settings
- Trust and adoption depend on UX design, not just AI capability
