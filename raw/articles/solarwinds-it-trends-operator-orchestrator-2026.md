# SolarWinds 2026 IT Trends Report: The Human Side of Autonomous IT

Released April 15, 2026. Surveyed 1,000+ IT professionals across operations, ITSM, leadership, app/platform engineering, security, and network operations. Partnership with UserEvidence.

**Headline finding:** 80% of respondents agree the IT role is shifting from operator to orchestrator.

**Four dimensions of the shift:**
- 52% say the role is becoming more strategic
- 52% say it's more automation-driven
- 47% say it's more cross-functional
- 41% say it's more oversight-focused

**Key challenges identified:** Trust in autonomous systems, governance of AI-managed infrastructure, skill transition management.

**Significance:** First large-scale industry survey using the exact "operator→orchestrator" framing. IT serves as leading indicator for knowledge workforce broadly — what IT pros experience now, other professions experience in 12-18 months.
