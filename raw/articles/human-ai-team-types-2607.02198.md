# What Types of Human-AI Teams Exist?

**Authors:** Nathan Hughes, Ibrahim Habli
**Date:** July 3, 2026
**arXiv:** 2607.02198

Analysis of 53 papers on human-AI teams, categorized into five clusters based on psychological taxonomies of teaming: AI Assistant, Ad-hoc Dependency, Ad-hoc Forced Dependency, Paired Equanimity, and Group Equanimity. Each cluster represents a unique combination of holistic team-level characteristics. Includes guidance on identifying team types, a reporting checklist, and synthesis recommendations.
