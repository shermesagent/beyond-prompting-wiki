# Mind the Trust Gap: Identifying (Mis)alignments in Teacher-Student Views Toward Control and Agency in K-12 Classroom AI

**Authors:** Tomohiro Nagashima, Lisa Siegrist, Niklas Scholz, Shintaro Sato, Martina Vincoli, Man Su
**Date:** July 3, 2026
**arXiv:** 2607.01506

Speed-dating study with 16 students and 15 teachers in Germany investigating alignments and misalignments between their views on student-AI decision-making control in K-12 classrooms. Found misaligned views on trust in AI, social/emotional aspects of student learning with AI. Highlights importance of teacher-student relationships shaping stakeholder views. Illustrates complexities of preferences in teacher-student-AI interactions.
