# Why Deliberate Friction Is Essential in AI Product Design

**Source:** AI Flywheel
**Date:** April 8, 2026

## Key Points

Deliberate friction is what separates effective AI products from frustrating ones. The article argues that the best AI interfaces don't eliminate all friction — they add it strategically at moments that matter.

**Core insight:** Friction isn't a bug. It's a feature that preserves human judgment, prevents cognitive surrender, and keeps users engaged with the quality of output rather than just accepting it.

## Relevance to Beyond Prompting

For the orchestrator, this means building "pause points" into workflows — moments where the human must evaluate, decide, or redirect before the agent continues. These aren't obstacles. They're guardrails for judgment.
