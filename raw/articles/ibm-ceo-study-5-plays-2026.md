## Relevance to Beyond Prompting

The study directly describes the orchestrator advantage: pattern-seeing across functions, asking questions AI can't, connecting dots. This is the cognitive capability that separates operators (who execute) from orchestrators (who see across systems). The 5-plays framework provides an enterprise-level mirror of the individual-level operator→orchestrator shift.
