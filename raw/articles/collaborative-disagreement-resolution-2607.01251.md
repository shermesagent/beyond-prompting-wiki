# Collaborative Disagreement Resolution for Scalable Oversight

**Authors:** Yuyang Jiang, Chacha Chen, Teng Wu, Liwen Sun, Han Liu, Shi Feng, Chenhao Tan
**Date:** July 3, 2026
**arXiv:** 2607.01251

Proposes disagreement resolution as an alternative to adversarial debate for AI oversight. Drawing on human mediation principles, AI agents collaboratively identify points of disagreement, examine evidence, and converge toward consensus or isolate the "crux" of disagreement. Achieves 62.1% judging accuracy vs. 49.2% for standard debate. Reframes oversight from adversarial persuasion to collaborative truth-seeking.
